Our free Pakistan salary calculator helps you find out exactly how much money you take home each month after all deductions. What Does This Calculator Include?

This tool covers all major salary deductions in Pakistan: Income Tax as per FBR progressive slabs, EOBI (Employees' Old-Age Benefits), Provident Fund at 8.33%, and Professional Tax based on your province. You can switch between tax years from 2021–22 all the way to the latest 2025–26 budget.

FBR Tax Slabs for 2025–26

The Finance Act 2025–26 brought significant relief to salaried individuals. The tax rate on income between Rs. 600,000 and Rs. 1.2 million was reduced from 5% to just 1%. Middle-income brackets also saw reductions. The new slabs range from 0% (up to Rs. 600,000) to 35% (above Rs. 4.1 million annually).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the income tax exemption limit in Pakistan for 2025-26? ▼

For salaried individuals, income up to Rs. 600,000 per year (Rs. 50,000/month) is completely tax-free under FBR tax slabs 2025-26.

How is income tax calculated on salary in Pakistan? ▼

Pakistan uses a progressive tax system. Your annual salary is divided into brackets (slabs). Each portion of income is taxed at a different rate. You do not pay the top rate on your full income — only on the portion that falls in each bracket.

What is EOBI deduction in salary? ▼

EOBI (Employees' Old-Age Benefits Institution) is a social security contribution. The employee pays Rs. 370 per month and the employer pays 5% of minimum wage. It provides pension benefits after retirement.

Is provident fund deducted from salary in Pakistan? ▼

Provident Fund (PF) is optional and depends on your employer's policy. Typically, 8.33% of basic salary is deducted and the employer matches the same. This money is returned to you when you leave the job or retire.

What is professional tax in Pakistan? ▼

Professional tax is a small provincial tax deducted from salaries. The amount depends on your salary range and province. For most employees it ranges from Rs. 200 to Rs. 1,200 per month.

How much tax is deducted from Rs. 100,000 salary in Pakistan 2025-26? ▼

On a Rs. 100,000 monthly salary (Rs. 1,200,000 annual), the tax is Rs. 6,000 per year = Rs. 500 per month under 2025-26 slabs. This is much lower than 2024-25 thanks to the rate reduction from 5% to 1% in that bracket.
